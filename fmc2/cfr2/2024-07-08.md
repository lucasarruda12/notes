## Definindo naturais

````
0 ≝ λx λy. y       | λf λx. x       | λf λx f⁰ x
1 ≝ λx λy. x y     | λf λx. fx      | λf λx f¹ x
2 ≝ λx λy  x (x y) | λf λx. f (f x) | λf λx f² x

succ  ≝ λn λx λy. x (n x y)
plus  ≝ Hw 
times ≝ Hw
````
